Our founder, Chas McSweatworth, had a vision. A vision about style and comfort. And freedom. Chas believed that armed with a sewing machine, some cotton and a dream he could change the American way of life and release slackers everywhere from the confines of their rigid dresswear. We're proud to carry that tradition today with our vast line of sweatpants, serving the citizens of the world who wish to be themselves regardless of their surroundings. Sweaty's. Improving people's lives everywhere, one drawstring at a time. From the couch to the grocery store to the seat on your next Southwest Airlines flight, we've got you covered.
